About Our Project

Mithilesha Analytics is a cutting-edge platform designed to help businesses predict sales with unprecedented accuracy using Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) neural networks. Our system analyzes historical sales data to identify patterns and generate reliable forecasts that enable informed decision-making.

Our dashboard provides intuitive visualizations of sales performance, comparing actual data against AI-driven predictions. The platform also features anomaly detection capabilities to identify unusual sales patterns that may require attention, giving you early warnings about potential issues or opportunities.

Whether you're a small business looking to optimize inventory or a large enterprise needing accurate forecasts for strategic planning, our LSTM-powered analytics solution delivers the insights you need.
